About Fix-It Hawaii

Kauai's honest, hardworking handyman team — serving homeowners and property managers since 2009.

Built on Honesty, Sustained by Craftsmanship

Fix-It Hawaii was founded in 2009 by Marcus Kahananui, a Kapaa native who grew up helping his grandfather maintain their family home on the North Shore. What started as word-of-mouth repairs for neighbors has grown into Kauai's most trusted handyman service — without losing the personal touch that started it all.

Our philosophy is simple: show up when we say we will, charge what we quoted, and do work that lasts. In Hawaii's climate, that means using materials and methods specifically chosen for tropical conditions — humidity-resistant drywall compounds, salt-tolerant fasteners, and paints formulated for high-moisture environments.

We serve all of Kauai — from Kapaa and Lihue to Princeville, Poipu, and Waimea. For larger renovation projects, we coordinate licensed subcontractors and manage the entire job so you only have one point of contact.
